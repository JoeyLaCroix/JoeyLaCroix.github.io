About Us:

Souther Surf Co. is a brand and a clothing company that was started for the average beachgoer. As a brand we embrace stylish modern clothing that anyone can wear. Not only will everyone look good in our clothing but they will also be giving back, 5% of every purchase gets donated to cleaning up the ocean. Make the right decision, and purchase from Souther Surf Co.!


How we donate back to the ocean:

When ever somebody purchases any apparel from Souther Surf Co., 5% of the revenue is donated to cleaning the ocean. This is a very pressing problem beacuse there is so much pollution and trash in the ocean. With the revenue made from these shirts anyone can contribute to helping our oceans. Be a problem solver, buy Southern Surf Co.
